What do we do?

The Sankalp Club, the LNMIIT, organises compelling discussions that opens up our hearts and mind to rebuild one's character. It helps us to realise the goodness within oneself, and inculcates benevolence and compassion towards others. These discussions bolster self confidence amongst youth and transforms one's perception towards life. Not only do we organise discussions, but also implement them through social service, general awareness events etc.,which leaves a huge impact in the lives of the people involved.

EVENTS

JANMASHTAMI

Janmashtami is a Hindu festival celebrated to commemorate the birth of Lord Krishna. The festivities typically involve decorating the campus with flowers and lights, and feasting on sweets and traditional cuisine.

MWEP

Teaching mess workers is a valuable endeavor that empowers them with knowledge and skills, improving their employability. Investing in their education, we benefit the workers themselves as well as the .

SAHYOG

Inviting children from nearby villages to cultural programs and giving them sweets fosters social harmony and inclusive values. This approach can instill empathy and a sense of responsibility in individuals and groups.

VILLAGE PROJECT

Every weekend, we visited a village to teach and empower the locals. Through our efforts, we aimed to improve their education and promote self-sufficiency, thereby creating a positive impact in their lives.

SLUM VISIT

Visiting a slum creates awareness of the living conditions and challenges faced by marginalized communities. It can also inspire individuals and groups to take action towards improving their lives.

SARASWATI PUJA

Saraswati Puja is a Hindu festival celebrated to honor the goddess of knowledge, music, and art. It is observed with prayers, offerings, and cultural programs in educational institutions and households across India.
