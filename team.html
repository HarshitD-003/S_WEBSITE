About

The Sankalp Club, the LNMIIT, organises compelling discussions that opens up our hearts and mind to rebuild one's character. It helps us to realise the goodness within oneself, and inculcates benevolence and compassion towards others. These discussions bolster self confidence amongst youth and transforms one's perception towards life. Not only do we organise discussions, but also implement them through social service, general awareness events etc.,which leaves a huge impact in the lives of the people involved.
